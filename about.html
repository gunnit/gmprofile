About Gregor Marić

From Big 4 consulting to AI leadership - transforming businesses through strategic automation

My Journey

With over 15 years of experience spanning Big 4 consulting firms and technology leadership roles, I've dedicated my career to helping organizations unlock their potential through strategic AI implementation and business automation.

My passion lies at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions. I believe that AI and automation should not just be buzzwords, but transformative tools that deliver measurable results and empower teams to focus on what truly matters.

Today, as the founder of PugliAI and a trusted advisor to hundreds of organizations, I continue to bridge the gap between complex AI concepts and real-world business applications, making these powerful technologies accessible to everyone.
